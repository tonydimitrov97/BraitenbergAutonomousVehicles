Detailed Description

A mobile entity in the Arena, capable of updating its own position and/or velocity when asked by the simulation.

All mobile entities must have a heading angle so that their orientation can be properly drawn by the GraphicsArenaViewer.

Since this is also a food class, many of its methods are intuitively virtual.
