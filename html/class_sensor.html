Detailed Description

Parent class for both of the sensors a robot can have.

LightSensors are what detect the proximity of the robot to the light. They make sure the robot follows its behavior

The LightSensors get notified by light and create readings based upon their proximity to the light.

FoodSensors are what detect the proximity of the robot to the food. They make sure the robot does not starve

The FoodSensors get notified by food and create readings based upon their proximity to the food.
