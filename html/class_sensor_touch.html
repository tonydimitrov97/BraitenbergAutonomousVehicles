Detailed Description

Class representing a touch sensor.

Touch or "bump" sensors are "active" when pressed. For our purposes, imagine a series of bump sensors around the perimeter of an entity. A collision will activate the sensor at a particular point of contact, which translates to an angle of contact.

SensorTouch can be observed for collision events.
